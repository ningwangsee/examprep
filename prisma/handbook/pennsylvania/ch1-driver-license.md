# Chapter 1: Driver License (Applying for a Learner's Permit)
Source: https://www.pa.gov/agencies/dmv/driver-services/pennsylvania-drivers-manual/online-drivers-manual/applying-for-a-learners-permit

## Application Requirements
- Minimum age: 16 years old
- Form DL-180 (Non-Commercial Learner's Permit Application)
- Health care provider completes medical section; physical exam date may not be more than 6 months prior to 16th birthday
- If under 18: parent/guardian signs Form DL-180TD (parental consent); if supervising adult cannot accompany, must sign before notary public
- If 18+: two proofs of residency required (utility bills, W-2 forms, tax records, current weapons permits, lease/mortgage documents)
- At Driver License Center, present: DL-180, DL-180TD (if under 18), original proof of date of birth/ID, signed Social Security card, applicable fee
- Acceptable ID: standard passport and passport card
- Social Security number will NOT be printed on learner's permit or driver's license

## Learner's Permit Validity
- Valid for one year from issue date printed on permit
- If permit expires or road test failed three times: use Form DL-31 to add/extend/replace

## Supervision Requirements (Junior Learner's Permit)
- Must drive under supervision of licensed driver at least 21 years old, OR parent/guardian/person in loco parentis/spouse 18+ with driver's license
- Supervising driver must sit in front seat
- May not carry more passengers than available seat belts

## Required Driving Experience (Under 18)
- At least 65 hours of supervised behind-the-wheel skill-building
- No less than 10 hours of nighttime driving
- At least 5 hours of bad weather driving
- Mandatory 6-month waiting period from permit issue date before road test
- Must provide signed certificate of completion of 65 hours

## Knowledge Test Rules
- Only ONE attempt per day, regardless of test location
- If failed: examiner returns DL-180 and DL-180TD forms for future attempts

## Vision Standards
- Poor vision in either eye may still qualify; may require glasses/contacts, daylight-only, or side-mirror-equipped vehicles
- If vision fails standards: DL-102 (Report of Eye Examination Form) issued

## Driver License Classes
- Class A (min. age 18): Any combination of vehicles with GVWR 26,001+ lbs where towed vehicle exceeds 10,000 lbs
- Class B (min. age 18): Any single vehicle rated over 26,000 lbs
- Class C (min. age 16): Standard license for any vehicles except Class M; may operate motor-driven cycle ≤50CC auto-trans, or 3-wheeled motorcycle with enclosed cab
- Class M (min. age 16): Motorcycles and motor-driven cycles; restriction "8" = motor-driven cycle only; restriction "9" = no two-wheeled motorcycles

## Junior Driver's License Restrictions
- Curfew: No driving 11 p.m. to 5 a.m., UNLESS parent/guardian/person in loco parentis/spouse 18+ is with you
  - Exceptions: public service, volunteer firefighting, employment (with proper documentation)
- Passenger restriction (first 6 months): only ONE non-immediate-family passenger under 18 without an adult present
- Passenger restriction (after 6 months): up to THREE non-immediate-family passengers under 18
  - Increased limit does NOT apply if junior driver was ever in a crash where partially/fully responsible, or convicted of any driving violation
- Mandatory 90-day suspension: if accumulate 6+ points OR convicted of driving 26+ mph over posted speed limit

## Upgrading Junior Driver's License
- Early upgrade (before 18): hold junior license 1 year, passed PA Dept of Education approved driver training course, no crashes/violations for 1 year, parental consent required; file Form DL-59
- Automatic upgrade: junior license becomes regular driver's license when driver turns 18

## New Residents
- Must obtain PA license within 60 days of moving to Pennsylvania; surrender out-of-state license
- Foreign license holders: may drive up to 1 year from entry date, or expiration of foreign license (whichever comes first)
- Reciprocal agreements: France, Germany, Korea, Taiwan (transfers from Puerto Rico, France, Germany cannot be same-day)
